01
Order Management System
The problem

Manual order processing was the single biggest bottleneck — slow, error-prone, and unable to scale with growing order volume.

What I did

Owned it end-to-end: identified the root cause, wrote the BRD, built and tested the prototype with the ops team, and drove org-wide adoption.

10× order volume, same team size

02
Procurement & Inventory
The problem

No structured flow existed from purchase order to stock — inventory visibility was reactive, not planned.

What I did

Designed the full flow: PO → billing → stock-in → QC categorisation, in partnership with Finance and Supply Chain.

REAL-TIME stock visibility, for the first time
STRUCTURED fulfilment planning
FASTER decision-making on inventory
